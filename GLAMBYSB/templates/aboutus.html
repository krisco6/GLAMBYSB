Who We Are

GLAMBYSB is a luxury beauty wholesale distributor dedicated to offering top-tier skincare, makeup, and haircare products at competitive prices.

Started Small, Dreaming Big

We began in a tiny room with one shelf of stock and a mission: to connect salons, retailers, and online sellers with authentic, high-quality beauty products. Today, GLAMBYSB serves hundreds of clients worldwide with a growing team and international suppliers.
